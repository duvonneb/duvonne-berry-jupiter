Duvonne Berry

About

Duvonne Berry, an IT professional with over a decade of experience in Florida, currently serves as the Network and Computer Systems Administrator for the Florida Department of Health in Lake County. He’s advanced from a tier 1 help desk technician to an IT manager role, showcasing his expertise and leadership. A Florida State University graduate with a Master’s in Information Technology, he’s known for his collaborative spirit. Outside of work, he enjoys connecting with friends visiting theme parks like Disney World and Universal Studios.
